Odin's Recipe

Welcome to my Recipe Website, with the help of The Odin Project Community, this site will serve as a Recipe book of mine curated from various filipino recipe websites.

Growing up, I always cherished the encyclopedia book my parents gifted to me as a child, before bedtime I would always flip over the pages of the book to the section where it shows all the different foods around the world.

That's why with this recipe book I would try my best to show you the recipes of foods which belongs a special place in my heart.
